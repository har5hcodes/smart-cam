InterviewBlindspots.com is a code review site with focus on software engineer interviews. Just like stackoverflow, you can paste your code and one of our volunteers will review the code. Watch this video to get started. You can view all the public code reviews so far here. Click on the links at the top to look at top reviews so far. Happy interviewing! our contributors.

Privacy Policy. As of now, we do not collect any private data except for email address. We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not even have a business model. All user contributions are licensed under cc by-sa 4.0 with attribution required. This means you are free to copy and share them. Just please provide an attribution by mentioning the link to original review. Currently v4.4.1. Code licensed MIT, docs CC BY 3.0.

As you can see, the looks of the site are not great. Thats because we are backend engineers with experience in managing 1000s of servers but (almost) no experience in frontend and its intricacies. We are looking for a frontend engineer who take this site to the next level. If you are interested, please contact us. Equity only!
